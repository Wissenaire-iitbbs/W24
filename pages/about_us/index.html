About Us

Wissenaire, the annual techno-management fest of IIT Bhubaneswar is one of the biggest of its kind in East India. This three-day event takes the participants into the world of cutting-edge innovations. Wissenaire adopts a new theme every year. Our theme this year is 'Pacem Strife'. Our motto in choosing this theme is to highlight the importance of defence technology as a deterrent to aggression and to promote national security. Defence technology is key to national security as it ensures a robust and advanced defence infrastructure. It acts as a crucial shield, fostering stability and deterring adversaries, ultimately contributing to the protection and sovereignty of a nation.
